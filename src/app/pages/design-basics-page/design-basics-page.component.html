Typography

CreamNg adopts a font stack of native font families in order to give you more flexibility and portability, as much as to gain in performance.

The native font families used in the stack are: 'SFNSText', 'HelveticaNeueDeskInterface', 'LucidaGrandeUI', 'Ubuntu', 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto', 'DroidSans', and 'Tahoma'.

You may add your own typeface through your customized style-sheet. Refer to the " Custom Design " page for more information on how to customize your design.
